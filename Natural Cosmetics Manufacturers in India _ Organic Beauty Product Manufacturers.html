RESEARCH &
DEVELOPMENT

We have state of art Research & Development Laboratory to
formulate any concept into finished product.
We are very competent in Designing Custom Formulations.

Though, we have laboratory facility we choose to stay away from tests which involve animal cruelty - like we don't conduct or promote Live Animal Dermatology Testing, Hypoallergenic and other tests which involve use of body parts and organs from animals slaughtered for meat industry.

We don't want to contribute towards the countless suffering of animals in laboratories for testing beauty products.

Hence, at Vert Laboratoire our objective is to achieve balance & harmony with nature, animals & humans.

We strive to make world beautiful without harming anyone in the entire value chain. So that it is Sustainable and keep changing lives positively.
